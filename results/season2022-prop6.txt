NOTE:
This is the schedule as generated by the aflg-matchplanner tool.

It has one imbalance: Berlin women play only three home matches,
whereas the other women teams play four, and the joint team plays five.
This can be resolved by moving the women match on the first match day
from Kiel to Berlin. This would then be the only instance where
a women team hosts a match day while their men team is playing away
(in Amsterdam, in this case).

Making this adjustment would bring every women team to 4 home matches,
balance out the travel distances, and there would also still be one match
of the joint team played in Kiel.
